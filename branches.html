Daleon Legal Solutions

Remote legal staffing and legal BPO for U.S. law firms. Bilingual teams trained in Personal Injury, Employment, Family Law, and other practice areas, integrated as a direct extension of your firm.

Roles we provide

  • Intake Specialists for new client calls and marketing leads.
  • Case Managers responsible for day-to-day case handling and client communication.
  • Paralegals & Legal Assistants with experience in PI, discovery, calendaring, and documentation.
  • Legal Admin & Clerks for repetitive tasks, file organization, and operational support.

What type of work do we cover?

  • Intake and case qualification (calls, forms, initial follow-up).
  • Case file management, legal CRM updates, and document organization.
  • Medical record requests, provider follow-up, and billing for records.
  • Drafting simple letters, summaries, timelines, and basic discovery support.
  • Ongoing client communication: reminders, updates, and expectation management.

Ideal for

  • High-volume Personal Injury firms.

  • Firms that need consistent support on records and follow-up.

Daleon Marketing Solutions

Nearshore digital marketing teams for firms, agencies, and businesses that need consistent presence, quality content, and performance campaigns without inflating their in-house headcount.

Core services

  • Social media management (content calendar, design, copy, and publishing).
  • Branding and graphic design for personal and corporate brands.
  • Paid ads on Meta, Google, LinkedIn, and other platforms.
  • Content creation: blog posts, landing pages, email sequences.
  • Video editing and motion graphics for ads and social media.

How we work with your brand

  • We build or refine your visual identity and tone of voice.
  • We design a content and campaigns calendar aligned with your goals.
  • We measure results (reach, leads, CPL, etc.) and iterate based on data.
