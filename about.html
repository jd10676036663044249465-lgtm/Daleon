Who we are

Daleon Group is a business ecosystem born in Colombia and strategically expanded into the U.S. market. We build a bridge of talent, technology, and structured processes between Latin America and the most demanding firms in the United States.

What started as a boutique project focused on legal staffing quickly evolved into a multidisciplinary conglomerate comprised of four specialized units:

  • Daleon Legal Solutions
  • Daleon Marketing & Sales Solutions
  • Daleon BPO Solutions
  • Daleon Tech Solutions

Our essence is simple yet powerful: develop talent, structure operations, and transform ordinary teams into extraordinary engines of growth.

What sets us apart

  • Specialized in U.S. law firms (PI, Employment, Lemon Law, and more)
  • Bilingual talent fully embedded in your tools and processes
  • Data-driven onboarding, training, and performance monitoring
  • Flexible engagement models aligned with your growth stage

Our Story & Expansion

  1. 01

    Born in Colombia

    Daleon was born in Colombia with a bold belief: Latin American talent — when trained with international standards, real leadership, and world-class technology — can compete directly in the most sophisticated markets in the world.

  2. 02

    Supporting growing firms

    We began by supporting small firms, solving urgent operational challenges in intake, case management, and back office work.

  3. 03

    Key expansion into the U.S.

    As clients placed their trust in us, we expanded into the U.S. market — bringing our methods, culture, and leadership-driven approach to a new level.

  4. 04

    From support to evolution

    Today, Daleon operates as a strategic nearshore growth partner that helps law firms, businesses, and startups scale without losing control. We don’t just support companies — we accelerate their evolution.
