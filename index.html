About Daleon Group

Daleon Group was created to connect Latin American talent with companies and law firms in North America, guided by leadership, excellence, efficiency, and human quality.

We operate as a nearshore holding with four units: Legal, Marketing, Sales, and BPO. Each unit has its own specialization, but we share a common standard of quality, training, and accountability.

Our goal is for every client to feel that their nearshore team is a natural extension of their organization.
